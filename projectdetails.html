Websites

Here is a bit of details on some of the work experiences on websites. All the websites developed are compatible with all screen sizes.

Supergreen DCD

The client wanted an informative website to market their innovative technology, but also a website that would be easy for people to understand their business.

Iskandar Chamber of Commerce

The client wanted a website for their company that would showcase their goals, their actions and their contributions to the society.

Wordpress Blog

This is a Wordpress site, my personal blog where I sometimes write articles as a hobby about travel destinations, food, fashion, lifestyle and health.

Orderutan

This startup was user based website and phone app. Consumers could order items from shopping malls and runners would deliver it to the preferred location. 

Leet Academy 1337 Ventures

I worked under Awan Ventures on this project. The client wanted a website for mentoring students where the students can add mentors and they can check their progress.

Bling Nightlife

This was a startup where clubs or bars would be able to put up events or concerts for marketing purposes and consumers could book the tickets to those events from the website.

DonorDrive

This was a non-profit startup regarding blood donation application and website where people would get notified of nearby emergencies and go donate blood if there is a shortage in a specific blood group.

University's marketing websites

I worked under Chamrun Digital for designing two websites for European Universities solely for marketing purposes of Malaysian study abroad programmes.

VR 

VR projects developed were for an experience that can also be used for advertising as well as understanding a scenario. Some projects are as follows:
1. Visualising a building with panoramic images and minor interactions.
2. Creating an experience of a science fiction lab for stereo-chemistry education and visualisation with interactions and speech.
3. Creating an experience of a story of a civil engineer's day in oil rig for educational and visualisation purposes with many interactions such as grabbing objects. 


Mobile Apps

The projects of mobile apps are as follows:
1. Orderutan: This startup was user based website and phone app. Consumers could order items from the malls and runners could check according to their location and decide to deliver the item to the consumer in their preferred location. I worked in this ordering startup in a dedicated group of 3. 
2. Pod by Prudential: I worked under Awan Ventures for this app on a part for Payment Gateway.
3. DonorDrive: This was a startup for location based blood donation application and website where people would get notified of nearby emergencies and go donate blood if there is a shortage in a specific blood group.
